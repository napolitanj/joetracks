- **Field Guides** ‚Äî Detailed, experience-driven writeups from real trips
- **Planning Tools** ‚Äî Browser-based utilities for faster decisions

---

## **What Lives Here**

### **Field Guides**

Long-form, structured guides built from direct experience:

- Route beta & terrain considerations
- Access & logistics notes
- Gear decisions & lessons learned
- Visual references tied directly to text

These are written to be useful for planning, not just inspirational.

---

### **Planning Tools**

Simple browser-based utilities designed to reduce friction:

- No accounts
- No installs
- Immediate usability

Example: **Michigan Ski Tracker**, which parses NOAA data to provide resort-specific snowfall estimates.

## **Philosophy**

JoeTracks prioritizes:

- **Clarity over hype**
- **Systems over noise**
- **Practicality over abstraction**

The goal is helping people spend more time outside with fewer bad assumptions.
